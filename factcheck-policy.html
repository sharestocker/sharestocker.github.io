Fact-Checking Policy

At sharestocker.in, we are dedicated to maintaining accuracy and trustworthiness across all our content. Our approach to fact-checking ensures our readers receive reliable information backed by solid evidence and transparency.

1. Commitment to Accuracy

  • We approach all claims with skepticism and rigor, seeking clarity and supporting evidence. Accuracy is fundamental to our credibility, so we ensure each piece is well-researched, relevant, and substantiated with reputable sources.
  • We disclose any constraints that may impact the accuracy of a report, ensuring our audience understands when information is tentative or evolving.

2. Evidence-Based Content

  • Our articles are thoroughly researched and based on reliable data, such as verified financial reports, market studies, and industry expert opinions. We avoid unsupported claims, speculation, and seek independent verification for critical information.
  • Claims made by individuals or organizations with potential biases are carefully corroborated, and, when necessary, attributed with appropriate context to maintain objectivity.

3. Transparency and Integrity

  • We do not knowingly mislead readers or distort facts, and we avoid using any information that cannot be independently verified. Our team ensures that facts are presented in a clear and accurate manner, aiming to build and maintain the trust of our audience.

4. Corrections Policy

  • If an error is identified, we act promptly to correct it. Our corrections process is transparent:
    • Online Articles: In cases of factual errors, we update the article and append a note clarifying the error and correction date.
    • Social Media: Any social media posts sharing corrected articles will be updated or reposted with a clear correction notice.
  • Readers can report inaccuracies by contacting us through our “Suggest A Correction” feature at the end of every article. We review all submissions, and if an error is confirmed, a correction will be issued promptly, and the reader will be notified.

Our team values our readers’ trust and strives for excellence in providing well-founded, accurate content. Thank you for your continued confidence in sharestocker.in.
