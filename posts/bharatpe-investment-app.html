BharatPe Investment App: Your Easy Guide to Smart Investing

Published on:

Introduction
BharatPe has taken a big step forward with its new Invest BharatPe app, making it easier for everyone to manage savings and investments all in one spot. This app introduces options like digital gold and fixed deposits, and soon, peer-to-peer (P2P) lending, giving users a chance to explore secure and straightforward financial choices. Let’s explore the key features and benefits that make this app a helpful financial tool for people at any stage of their investment journey.

Top Features of the BharatPe Investment App

1. Easy-to-Use P2P Lending Option

Included in the app is BharatPe's P2P lending feature, previously known as the “12% Club.” For now, however, new investments in this feature are paused due to updated RBI guidelines. Once it’s back, the goal is to bring more security and ease for everyone interested in lending directly to others.

2. Secure Fixed Deposit Options

Want to start saving with guaranteed returns? The app offers fixed deposits (FDs) through trusted financial partners. You only need ₹1,000 to get started, making it easy for anyone to begin saving and growing their money in a safe way.

3. Invest in Digital Gold

For those interested in gold investments without the hassle of storage, Invest BharatPe partners with Safegold to let users buy digital gold. This option gives you the benefits of owning gold without needing to keep it physically, and you can redeem it anytime.

4. Improved Financial Stability

BharatPe has been working hard on its financial growth, which shows in its increasing revenue and reduced losses. This improvement reflects the company's commitment to building a secure and successful financial app for its users.

5. Expanding as a “Super App”

Invest BharatPe is becoming more than just a payments app—it’s evolving into a “super app.” This means it will soon offer a variety of financial services, including payments, lending, and other investment products, all in one place for easier access and use.

6. New Subsidiary for Smooth Lending Operations

To keep its lending operations streamlined, BharatPe has created a new branch called Resilient Digi Services Private Limited. This structure provides stronger support and better management for the app’s financial services.

7. Resolution with Co-Founder

Recently, BharatPe resolved its legal dispute with co-founder Ashneer Grover. With this matter settled, the company is now fully focused on growth and delivering valuable features to users.

Conclusion

The Invest BharatPe app is an easy-to-use platform that combines saving and investment options into one app. With choices like fixed deposits, digital gold, and soon, a safe P2P lending product, it covers many financial needs. BharatPe’s growth, strong commitment to compliance, and recent improvements make this app a great choice for both new and seasoned investors.
